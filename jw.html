Jehovah's Witnesses- Who are we?



We come from hundreds of ethnic and language backgrounds, yet
we are united by common goals. Above all, we want to honor
jehovah, the God of the Bible and the creator of all things. we do
our best to imitate jesus christ and are proud to be called
Christians. Each of us regularly spends time helpinng people learn
about the Bible and Gods's kingdom. Because we witness, or talk,
about jehovah God and his kingdom. we are known as Jehovah's
Witnesses.
